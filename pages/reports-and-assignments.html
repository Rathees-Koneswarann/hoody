Grammarly

Grammarly is a cloud-based typing assistant that reviews spelling, grammar, punctuation, clarity, engagement, and delivery mistakes in text. It uses artificial intelligence to identify and search for an appropriate replacement for the located error. You can use it for free in

  • Chrome extension
  • Windows
  • iPhone and iPad
  • Android

Grammarly helps you write mistake-free in Gmail, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and any other app you use. Even in text messages! and I personally suggest Grammerly to students because it particularly effective at correcting errors of students whose first language is not English.

you can use the free version of Grammarly for as long as you want.

QuillBot

Quillbot is an AI-based writing tool that has several core functionalities. Its primary purpose is as a paraphrasing tool that uses artificial intelligence to reword your writing in a number of different styles. You can use it as

  • Paraphrasing Tool
  • Grammar Checker
  • Plagiarism Checker
  • Summarizer

The free version of Quillbot allows you to paraphrase up to 125 words, and limits the paraphrasing styles available. You can summarize up to 1200 words of research.

CamScanner

CamScanner is a Chinese mobile app that allows iOS and Android devices to be used as image scanners. It allows students to 'scan' documents and share the photo as either a JPEG or PDF. This app is available free of charge on the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store. Just take a photo, and CamScanner take care of the rest.

  • Scan any kind of document in seconds, texts or graphics.
  • Scan multi-page documents
  • Batch scan documents
  • Auto-crop scanned photos
  • Smart-enhance the scan results
  • Customize PDF page size: 10 page size options (Letter, A4, B5 and etc.)

CamScanner allows you to use your phone camera to scan and digitize all kinds of paper documents: receipts, notes, invoices, whiteboard discussions, business cards, certificates,etc.

It has smart cropping and auto enhancing to ensure the texts and graphics in scanned documents are clear and sharp with premium colours and resolutions..

LibreOffice

LibreOffice is a free and open-source document suite that's available on all major OSes, but it still doesn't offer online access or collaboration features. I personally suggest this tool for students to edit pdf documents. It changes pdf document to edit like a MS Word document(in pdf format). There are a lot of features in this free app in pdf editing.

  • Tagged PDF
  • Time stamp protocol⁠
  • Detailed image settings
  • Hybrid PDF
  • Eextended security and permission options
  • PDF forms
  • Initial view settings
  • Extended handling of URLs
  • Digital signatures

More than above you can use this app as,

  • LibreOffice Writer (like MS Word)
  • LibreOffice Calc (like MS Excel)
  • LibreOffice Impress (like MS Power Point)
  • LibreOffice Draw (Like Adope Acrobat)
